FSG stands for "Filtered Seed Glitchless". It's a speedrunning category where runners use a random geerator of seeds with favorable structures and conditions, making it easier to complete runs quickly compared to Random Seed Glitchless (RSG).

This generator gives seeds for Minecraft Bedrock Edition 1.16.1 with specific structures at optimal locations. Each mode filters for different combinations of structures to suit various playstyles and trains different aspect of speedrunning, such as playing with little food, quick decision making, etc.
